About the Servant

Pastor & Community Worker

Welcome. In His Presence Prayer Altar is a personal initiative through which I seek to connect, pray, and support individuals outside the formal church setting. While I serve as a pastor with the PICC family, this platform allows me to engage with people as a pastor in a more personal capacity.

My Journey

I believe that ministry extends beyond church walls. As a community worker by profession, I aim to address both spiritual and social needs, offering guidance, prayer, and practical support wherever it is needed.

"To serve God is to serve humanity."
